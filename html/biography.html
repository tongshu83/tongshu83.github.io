Biography

Dr. Tong Shu joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of North Texas as an assistant professor in August 2022. Before that, she was an assistant professor in the School of Computing at Southern Illinois University Carbondale from August 2020 to August 2022, and worked as a postdoctoral appointee under the supervision of Dr. Justin M. Wozniak and Prof. Ian T. Foster in Data Science and Learning/Mathematics and Computer Science Division at Argonne National Laboratory from October 2017 to August 2020.

Dr. Tong Shu was honored with the 2025 UNT CSE Junior Faculty Research Award.
